About Us

  • Broadcasting faith, education, health, music & campus life from Ilishan-Remo.
  • Focus on student training in broadcasting, campus news, faith-based & health-oriented content, community engagement, and uplifting music/programming for youths.
  • Supports Adventist World Radio ties, faith integration

Our Mission

To uphold Babcock University as a center of excellence through the development of excellent employees and students, quality service delivery in teaching, learning, institutional research, environmental management, and biblical spirituality.

Core Values

  • Trust in God
  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Diligence
  • Persistence
  • Service
  • Loyalty to Seventh-day Adventist Heritage.
